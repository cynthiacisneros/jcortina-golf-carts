Our Story

J. Cortina Golf Carts started with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable golf cart repair and service to the Mission, TX community and surrounding Rio Grande Valley area.

With years of hands-on experience working on every make and model of golf cart, we've built a reputation for quality workmanship and fair pricing. We understand that your golf cart is more than just a vehicle — it's how you get around the course, the neighborhood, and beyond.

Whether you need a quick battery replacement, a motor rebuild, or a full tune-up, we treat every cart like it's our own. No shortcuts, no upselling — just honest work done right.
