Our Services

Complete golf cart care — from quick fixes to full repairs.

🔋

Battery Replacement & Maintenance

We diagnose, replace, and maintain all types of golf cart batteries — including 6V, 8V, and 12V systems. Keep your cart powered and performing at its best.

  • Battery testing & diagnostics
  • Full battery replacement
  • Terminal cleaning & corrosion prevention
  • Water level checks & refills
⚡

Motor & Controller Repair

Whether your cart is sluggish or won't move at all, we troubleshoot and repair motors, controllers, and drive systems for all major brands.

  • Motor diagnostics & rebuilds
  • Controller testing & replacement
  • Speed controller adjustments
  • Solenoid & wiring checks
🔉

Tire & Wheel Services

From flats to full wheel upgrades, we keep your cart rolling smoothly on any terrain.

  • Tire replacement (all sizes)
  • Tube repair & replacement
  • Wheel alignment
  • Hub & bearing service
🔌

Electrical & Wiring

Electrical issues can be tricky. We track down shorts, bad connections, and faulty components to get your cart back in action.

  • Full electrical diagnostics
  • Wiring harness repair
  • Lighting & accessory installation
  • Fuse & relay replacement
🛠️

General Maintenance & Tune-ups

Regular maintenance keeps your cart reliable. We offer complete tune-ups to catch small issues before they become big problems.

  • Brake inspection & adjustment
  • Fluid level checks
  • Lubrication & greasing
  • Full safety inspection
